How to Use


Set up is easy, click the create an acount button and provide an email address and create your password. Then simple personal event creation is at your finger tips.

Making an event


Log in to your account, click make event, and then enter in your events details. Once you click create event a link to your new event will be generated and you can send it to all your Friends on whatever social media platforms they use!
